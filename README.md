## What is Murmur?

[Murmur](https://murmurtts.com/) is a native macOS text-to-speech studio that runs entirely on your Mac. Paste text, pick a voice, hit Generate, and Murmur outputs a WAV or M4A file. Everything happens on-device using Apple's MLX framework. No cloud calls, no telemetry, no per-character fees.

It's built for creators who need to produce real audio: podcasts, [audiobooks](https://murmurtts.com/use-cases), YouTube voiceovers, newsletter audio, e-learning narration, accessibility tooling.

## Why Murmur

- **Truly offline.** Your text, audio, and cloned voices never leave your Mac. No accounts, no API keys, no usage logs.
- **One price, unlimited use.** $49 one-time. No subscription, no per-character cost, no usage caps. [See pricing →](https://murmurtts.com/#pricing)
- **Real production output.** WAV and M4A export, batch processing for long documents, speed control, and chapter splitting for audiobook workflows.
- **Apple Silicon native.** 3x real-time generation on M-series Macs via MLX. No Rosetta, no GPU gymnastics.

## Features

- **[860+ voices](https://murmurtts.com/voices)** across [25+ languages](https://murmurtts.com/languages) including English, Spanish, French, German, Japanese, Korean, Mandarin, Hindi, Arabic, and more
- **Voice cloning** from a 10-second sample. Clone your own voice for show intros or character work.
- **Four AI models** in one app: [Kokoro](https://murmurtts.com/voices), Qwen3-TTS, Fish S2 Pro, and Chatterbox. Pick the right model for the job.
- **Batch processing** for long documents. Queue scripts, chapter files, or full novels and walk away.
- **Speed control** from 0.5x to 2x without pitch artifacts.
- **Native audio player** for instant preview before export.
- **Privacy by default.** No cloud, no account, no telemetry. [Read the privacy policy →](https://murmurtts.com/privacy)

## FAQ

**Does Murmur send my text to the cloud?**
No. All synthesis runs on your Mac. The app makes zero outbound network requests for generation. [Privacy details →](https://murmurtts.com/privacy)

**Can I use Murmur output commercially?**
Yes. You own the audio you generate.

**How does voice cloning work?**
Drop a 10-second clean voice sample into Murmur. It builds a reusable voice profile you can use forever. The sample and profile stay on your Mac.

**Will it work on Intel Macs?**
No. Murmur uses Apple's MLX framework, which requires Apple Silicon.
